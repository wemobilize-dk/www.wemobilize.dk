About me

I am a curious maverick with 20 years of experience in IT and a background in physics. I have done most things in IT and now I focus on utilizing my unique skills in the intersection between business cases, people and technology to help creating change by technology where it makes sense working with good people.

I deliver what I call CTO as a Service (CaaS).

Perhaps it’s more to the point to call it Nikolaj as a service since CTO can be many different things in an organisation. And really we need to know each other before I should start, so really you engage with me in a part time consulting role where I do a mixture of roles not necessarily hired for in one person in a large company, but quite efficient (both cost wise and impactwise, if I should voice my opinion ;-).
